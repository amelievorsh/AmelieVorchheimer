Conceptually speaking

let the glitches wash over you.


This is a space where creativity and technology collide. A space where I distort information, experiences, and perception to generate new layers of meaning.

The cloud is a space where I exist without bounds, without definition. Multiplicity as a concept embodies the idea that the self is not fixed, but rather in a state of flux. I engage the power of employing online space to reject colonial structures of singularity.

We exist in a flux of identities and realities both AFK and digitally. Embracing these identity fluxes and encouraging existing in the in-between, through refusing categorization, am I an artist? am I coder? am I a designer? am I a computer?

“individual attention forms the basis for collective attention and thus for meaningful refusal of all kinds.”(Odell, 2019:75). Thus, acts of refusal (being acknowledged) are contingent on a society adhering to the status quo.

Acknowledge the quiet acts of acting unexpectedly as a tool to disrupt. Embrace the third space.
